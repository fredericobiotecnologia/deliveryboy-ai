Real Operational Intelligence

Powered by real logistics behavior, not synthetic assumptions.

DeliveryBoy AI is powered by a real operational dataset extracted and exported from a WhatsApp delivery community in Patos de Minas, Brazil.

The community has 922 members, including local businesses and motorcycle couriers. The historical dataset spans March 2024 to July 2026 (more than two years) and contains real delivery requests, courier availability and operational demand patterns.

Unlike synthetic datasets, the AI learns from real logistics behavior observed in daily operations.

Instead of sending a motorcycle immediately to a distant neighborhood, DeliveryBoy AI analyzes more than two years of operational history and estimates whether another nearby delivery is likely to appear soon. When the probability is high, the AI recommends grouping deliveries into a single trip, reducing fuel costs, travel distance and idle time while increasing productivity for small businesses with only one or two motorcycle couriers.
